# 17. Recipe Builder

You are given a recipe builder with servings and ingredient lines.

Implement:

1. Adding ingredients from a free-text line such as `2 tbsp olive oil`.
2. Scaling ingredient quantities when servings change.
3. Editing or removing existing ingredients.
4. Validation for malformed ingredient lines.

Notes:

- You may choose your own internal ingredient model.
- Be ready to discuss how strict the parser should be in an interview setting.
