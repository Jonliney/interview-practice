# 04. Product Table

You are given a product table with a few useful controls missing.

Implement:

1. Sorting by `name`, `price`, or `stock`.
2. Row selection with a selected count.
3. A `Select visible` or `Select all visible` action.
4. A derived total price for selected rows.

Potential issues:

- The starter sorting approach is likely to mutate source data.
- The selection model is intentionally incomplete.
- Be ready to explain whether selection should survive sorting and filtering.
